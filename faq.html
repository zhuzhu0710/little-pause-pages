About Little Pause Pages

Little Pause Pages is a collection of toddler-friendly coloring books and printables designed for children ages 1–4. Our pages feature simple, engaging designs that support early learning, fine motor development, and creative play.

Little Pause Pages was created by parents and educators who understand the importance of calm, creative moments in early childhood. We believe every toddler deserves engaging activities that support their development.

Little Pause Pages is designed for toddlers ages 1–4. Our designs are simple enough for younger toddlers to enjoy while still engaging enough for older toddlers and preschoolers.

Our books are digital printables. You download them as PDF files and print them at home. This means instant access, no shipping delays, and the ability to print as many copies as you need.
